Humanity's Evolution

Humanity evolves from touch to clicks, from atoms to codes, transforming knowledge and identity. In this transmission phase, via design, C2W2 Runway tells a six-chapter story about our relationship with tech.

Tech's Impact on Art

How it first changes art, then devalues it through mass production, and finally forces a search for new meaning and identity. The click is the new biology.

C2W2 Overview

C2W2 is a 30-minute browser-based runway. No downloads, no logins. Out of seventy-five, selected forty-eight avatars appear in a six-chapter sequence, with hyper-real textile simulations and a clear narrative spine.

Merging Disciplines

The work merges digital fashion, architecture, and performance into one continuous environment. Each chapter marks a step in the evolution of identity, material, and medium in virtual culture.

World Insights

The metaverse space is a minimalist, dark environment with dramatic circular lighting, evoking a digital void where avatars evolve. Avatars rotate 360 degrees and walk in 15 meter radius loops during 30 seconds each.

Introducing C2W2 Virtual Runway 2025

C2W2 Virtual Runway, 2025 for 3D Beings and Agents. A 30 min. browser-based, on-chain experience with no login, no download—just click. Each chapter builds on the last: starting with AI-reimagined art classics, shifting to experimental digital fashion, blending NFTs and figures, resetting with pure AI generation, ascending minimally, and rebirth through transformed echoes.

Technical Milestones

Originally built on Hyperfy v1. Rebuilt using Three.js. Mintable as an HTML-based NFT. All files used in the show are stored on Arweave (PFADI), with the NFT minted on Ethereum.

First time ever that a metaverse experience itself has been directly minted and can be watched straight from OpenSea or any browser window. First time ultra-realistic, dynamic textile physics from Clo3D has been successfully exhibited in a standard web browser environment.
